### 🛰️ THE ORBITAL BROADCAST: HOW THIS WORKS
Every verse listed below translates real astronomical research I personally conducted—including tracking planet transits for **Exoplanet Watch** or classifying stars and galaxies on the **Zooniverse** network. 

To complete this physical-to-orbital transmission, a digital rendering of our obsidian-black master coin matrix is broadcast directly into outer space—uplinking via radio waves to **SAT GUS**, an active 12U CubeSat satellite orbiting 500km above Earth.

**The Space Camera Process:**
1. The satellite displays the minimalist reverse face (the back design) of our coin on a physical **Google Pixel smartphone screen** nested inside its orbital chassis (its protective space casing).
2. An onboard aerospace camera snaps a physical photograph of the screen.
3. The **literal curvature, cloud systems, and oceans of Planet Earth** serve as the backdrop!

*Note: While the master graphic remains a uniform constant—just like a repeating cosmic radio signal—the shifting clouds of Earth provide a completely unique, unrepeatable timestamp for each scheduled downlink.*

You can independently verify live tracking details via the [SAT GUS Space Registry (NORAD ID: 55432)](https://satnogs.org).
